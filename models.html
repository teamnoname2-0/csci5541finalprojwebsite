Ensemble Model Weights

Our fraud detection system uses an ensemble of three fine-tuned transformer models. Each model was trained on the same merged dataset (~28,000 job postings) and their predictions are combined via weighted voting for improved robustness.

LUKE (Primary Model)

Language Understanding with Knowledge-based Embeddings. Entity-aware transformer with specialized architecture for span-level representations. Used for evidence span extraction.

View on Hugging Face

RoBERTa

Robustly Optimized BERT Pretraining Approach. A robust variant of BERT with improved training methodology.

View on Hugging Face

DistilBERT

A distilled version of BERT that is smaller and faster while retaining most of BERT's performance.
